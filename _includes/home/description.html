UTBadgeChain

Longhorn Innovation Fund for Technology

2016 - 2017

The UTBadgeChain project is supported by the Longhorn Innovation Fund for Technology (LIFT), a fund of approximately $500,000 that is awarded by the University of Texas Research & Education Technology Committee (R&E) to innovate academic technology projects that leverage information technology in order to imporve quality of instruction, create a differentiator for attracting higher caliver students to the University, or result in a competitive advantage to the University in attracting sponsored research.
